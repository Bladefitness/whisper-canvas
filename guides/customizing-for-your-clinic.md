# Customizing for Your Clinic

How to adapt the training content for your specific practice, specialty, and brand.

---

## What to Customize

The training content uses generic clinic examples. To make it yours, swap these elements:

### 1. Specialty-Specific Examples

Replace the generic examples with ones from your specialty:

| Generic Example | Chiropractic | Physical Therapy | Med Spa | Dental |
|-----------------|-------------|------------------|---------|--------|
| "Back pain patient" | "Sciatica patient" | "Post-ACL patient" | "Aging skin client" | "TMJ patient" |
| "Sleep through the night" | "Sit at your desk pain-free" | "Run your first 5K" | "Look 10 years younger" | "Eat what you love again" |
| "$497 package" | "Spinal Correction Program" | "Return to Sport Protocol" | "Glow Up Package" | "Smile Transformation" |

### 2. Your Offer Names

Use the naming formula from Training 3:

**[Dream Result] + [Time Frame] + [Container Word]**

Examples by specialty:
- Chiro: "The 8-Week Spinal Correction Program"
- PT: "The 12-Week Return to Sport Protocol"
- Med Spa: "The 90-Day Total Glow Transformation"
- Dental: "The Complete Smile Makeover System"
- Wellness: "The Executive Vitality Optimization Protocol"

### 3. Your Numbers and Data

Replace placeholder statistics with your real data:
- Your actual patient outcomes ("87% of our patients...")
- Your review count ("200+ five-star reviews")
- Your response time ("We call within 3 minutes")
- Your pricing and package values

### 4. Clinical Sidebar Analogies

The clinical sidebars use general medical concepts. You can add specialty-specific ones:

| Specialty | Clinical Analogy | Marketing Concept |
|-----------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Chiropractic | Subluxation model | Hidden bottlenecks in your funnel |
| PT | Progressive overload | Scaling ad spend gradually |
| Dental | Preventive vs. restorative | TOFU (prevention) vs. BOFU (treatment) |
| Med Spa | Skin barrier function | Brand trust as a barrier to competitors |

## How to Edit the Training Scripts

### In Your Editor

1. Open any `training.md` file
2. Use Find & Replace (`Cmd+H` / `Ctrl+H`) to swap terms:
   - "clinic" → your practice type
   - "patient" → your preferred term (client, member, guest)
   - "back pain" → your specialty's common complaint
3. Update the example offers, prices, and packages
4. Save

### On GitHub

1. Click the pencil icon on any file to edit directly
2. Make your changes
3. Commit with a descriptive message

## How to Edit the Excalidraw Canvases

### Changing Text

1. Open the `.excalidraw` file in your editor
2. Double-click any text element to edit it
3. Update the text with your content
4. The canvas saves automatically

### Swapping Images

1. Generate new images using the prompts in [Generating Visuals](generating-visuals.md)
2. Open the canvas
3. Delete the existing image (click + Delete)
4. Drag your new image onto the canvas
5. Resize and position it in the frame

### Changing Colors

To match your brand colors:
1. Select elements you want to recolor
2. Use the color picker in the left panel
3. Update stroke and/or background colors

---

## Branding Tips

1. **Pick 2-3 brand colors** and use them consistently across all canvases
2. **Use your practice name** in titles instead of generic "clinic"
3. **Add your logo** — drag it onto the canvas as an image in the title bar area
4. **Update the footer** with your website or social handles
5. **Keep the clinical sidebars** — they're the secret weapon that makes clinic owners say "this person gets me"
