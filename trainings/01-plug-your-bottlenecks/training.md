# PLUG YOUR BOTTLENECKS
## The Clinic Owner's Growth System — Video Training Script

---

## INTRO: THE GOAL (0:00 - 1:30)

The ultimate goal of any clinic business is simple:

**Get great results → Increase revenue → Reinvest into the business → Help more people → Get more revenue**

That's the virtuous cycle. But here's the thing most people miss:

**You MUST start with reputation.** Good news travels, but bad news travels FASTER. If you have a bad reputation, it doesn't matter how much marketing you do — your business will implode from the inside out.

So step one: **Dial in your service delivery.** Make sure you're getting incredible results for your clients. Once that foundation is solid, NOW we build the system.

The system I'm going to walk you through is about taking your ideal prospects and turning them into ideal clients through a **predictable process** that gets them to know you, like you, and trust you.

---

## SECTION 1: THE 3-5% PROBLEM (1:30 - 3:00)

Here's where most clinic owners go wrong:

They just advertise. "Come get my offer. Come get my offer." Over and over.

Here's the problem: **Only 3-5% of people are EVER in a position to buy at any given time.**

That means if all your marketing is "buy now" messaging, you're fighting over a tiny sliver of the market — and so is every other clinic on your block.

That's a losing battle.

**The 95% is where the real opportunity is.** These are people who:
- Know they have a problem
- Are researching solutions
- Need more information before they commit
- Aren't ready TODAY, but will be ready in 30, 60, 90 days

The winners aren't fighting over the 3-5%. They're **nurturing the 95%** so that when those people ARE ready, there's only one obvious choice.

---

## SECTION 2: BE THE EDUCATOR, NOT THE ADVERTISER (3:00 - 5:00)

The way to win is to be **the educator.** The entertainer. The person who stands out and does things differently.

Here's a homework assignment: Go look at the top-viewed content on Facebook, TikTok, and Instagram — specifically in your niche. Study it.

What you'll find is that the content that gets millions of views isn't "Come buy my thing." It's content that:
- **Educates** — teaches people something they didn't know
- **Entertains** — makes them stop scrolling
- **Empathizes** — speaks to their exact struggles and pain points

So do the research. Figure out:
- What do your ideal clients need to know?
- What are their biggest issues and struggles?
- What questions are they typing into Google at 2am?

Then **create content around THAT.**

Now you're tapping into the middle of the funnel — the 95% who need nurturing. You give them something of value, for free or very low cost, and it takes them one step closer to your higher-ticket offer.

That's how you build trust at scale.

---

## SECTION 3: LEAD MAGNETS — Capture the Middle (5:00 - 6:30)

Once you understand what your audience needs, you create **lead magnets.**

A lead magnet is something of value you give away for free (or very low cost) in exchange for their contact information:
- A free guide: "5 Things to Do Before Your First Visit"
- A quiz: "Which Treatment Is Right For You?"
- A video series: "3 Exercises to Start Today"
- A free consultation or assessment

The lead magnet does two things:
1. It **captures** the 95% who aren't ready to buy yet
2. It **positions you** as the expert and the obvious choice when they ARE ready

But here's where most people stop. They get the lead... and then they lose them.

**You have to plug the holes.** Every stage of your funnel where people fall out is a bottleneck that's costing you money.

That means you need to play **offense AND defense.**

---

## SECTION 4: PLAY OFFENSE — Speed to Lead (6:30 - 8:30)

**Offense = human outreach. Fast.**

When a lead comes in — someone fills out a form, calls your clinic, responds to an ad — you need a dedicated person whose ENTIRE JOB is to:

1. **Call that lead within 5 minutes.** Not an hour. Not tomorrow. FIVE MINUTES.
2. **Follow up every single day for at least 7 days.** Most leads don't answer the first call. Or the second. The fortune is in the follow-up.
3. **Keep following up** — with decreasing frequency — essentially until they book or tell you to stop.

This is not a "when I get a chance" task. This is someone's PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITY. If no one owns it, it doesn't happen. And every lead that goes uncalled is money you already spent to acquire — walking out the door.

The data is clear: **Speed to lead is the #1 predictor of conversion.** The clinic that calls first wins.

### 🩺 Clinical Sidebar: TRIAGE Your Leads

You already know this concept — you do it every day in the clinic.

**Emergency triage:** Red (immediate), Yellow (urgent), Green (can wait). You'd never treat a sprained ankle before a STEMI.

Your leads work the same way:
- **🔴 Red leads (HOT):** Filled out a form in the last 5 minutes. Call NOW. This is the chest pain patient — every minute of delay is tissue death.
- **🟡 Yellow leads (WARM):** Downloaded your guide, opened 3 emails. Nurture actively. They're the unstable patient — needs monitoring and intervention.
- **🟢 Green leads (COOL):** Followed you on IG, watched a reel. Keep providing content. They're the waiting room patient — they'll declare when ready.

**You triage in the ER without thinking. But in your business, you treat a 5-minute-old lead the same as a 3-day-old one. You'd NEVER do that with a patient.**

---

## SECTION 5: PLAY DEFENSE — Automation (8:30 - 10:30)

**Defense = systems that work when you can't.**

Let's be honest: No one is sitting at a computer all day, glued to the screen, texting leads back and forth. That's not a great use of anyone's time. And you don't have all the answers memorized for every question.

So you build systems that do it for you.

When a new lead reaches out, an **automated system** should immediately:
- "Hey! Thanks for reaching out. How can I help?"
- "What are you looking for?"
- "Would you like to lock in our current special?"
- "When's a good time to come in?"

This should happen **automatically** in 2026. No human should be doing this manually.

The AI handles the initial conversation, answers common questions, qualifies the lead, and books the appointment. You and your team **monitor and oversee** — jumping in only when needed.

This isn't about replacing humans. It's about **freeing humans to do what only humans can do** — build relationships, deliver great care, close high-value clients.

---

## SECTION 6: 24/7 AVAILABILITY — The Unfair Advantage (10:30 - 12:00)

Here's the one that most clinic owners are sleeping on — literally.

**When your business is closed, you should be the ONLY business on the block that can still receive and respond to inquiries. By phone. 24 hours a day. 7 days a week.**

How? An **after-hours AI voice bot** that:
- Answers every phone call after hours
- Sounds natural and professional
- Answers common questions about services, pricing, hours
- Takes notes and messages
- Schedules appointments directly into your calendar

Think about what happens right now: Someone has back pain at 9pm. They Google "chiropractor near me." They call 5 clinics. 4 go to voicemail. YOUR clinic picks up, answers their questions, and books them for tomorrow morning.

**That is an uneatable unfair advantage.**

This is available RIGHT NOW. But it won't be an advantage forever — eventually everyone will have it. The early movers win.

---

## SECTION 7: UPSELL & BUNDLE — Maximize Revenue Per Visit (12:00 - 13:30)

Last piece of the puzzle: **maximizing revenue from every client who walks through your door.**

When someone comes in for one service, ask yourself:
- Is there something else that could genuinely benefit them?
- Can you bundle services together for a better outcome AND higher ticket?

Example: Someone comes in for an adjustment. Could they also benefit from soft tissue work? A corrective exercise plan? A supplement protocol? A maintenance membership?

**Bundling does two things:**
1. You deliver MORE value (better outcomes = better reputation = more referrals)
2. You stop competing in a race to the bottom on single-service pricing

Instead of being the cheapest adjustment in town, you're offering a **comprehensive solution** that no one else packages the way you do.

That's how you compete on VALUE, not price.

---

### 🩺 Clinical Sidebar: YOUR BUSINESS HAS VITAL SIGNS

You check vitals on every patient: HR, BP, RR, Temp, SpO2. If one is abnormal, you investigate. You don't wait until the patient codes.

**Your business has vital signs too:**

| Vital Sign | Business KPI | "Normal Range" |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| Heart Rate | Lead Volume | 50-200/month |
| Blood Pressure | Cost Per Lead | <$50 |
| Temperature | Click-Through Rate | >1.5% |
| SpO₂ | Conversion Rate | >15% |
| Respiratory Rate | Speed to Lead | <5 minutes |

**You'd never skip vitals on a patient. But most of you have NO IDEA what your CPL was last month.** Check your business vitals daily, or you'll code and not know why.

---

### 🩺 Clinical Sidebar: IATROGENIC MARKETING

Sometimes the treatment causes harm. Unnecessary surgery. Antibiotic resistance. Opioid dependence. In medicine we call it **iatrogenic** — harm caused by the healer.

**Bad marketing doesn't just fail — it actively HURTS you:**
- **Spammy ads** → damage brand reputation (iatrogenic injury)
- **Slow follow-up** → teaches leads you don't care (negligence)
- **Constant discounting** → trains patients to only buy on sale (dependence)
- **Over-promising results** → angry patients who leave bad reviews (adverse reaction)

*"First, do no harm" applies to your marketing too. A bad ad isn't neutral — it's iatrogenic. It's making your brand sicker.*

## CLOSING: THE BOTTLENECK AUDIT (13:30 - 15:00)

So here's your framework. Seven stages. Each one is a potential bottleneck:

| Stage | Question to Ask |
|-------|----------------|
| 1. Reputation | Are we consistently getting great results? |
| 2. Content | Are we educating the 95%, or just advertising to the 5%? |
| 3. Lead Magnets | Do we have something valuable to capture leads? |
| 4. Speed to Lead | Are we calling every lead within 5 minutes? |
| 5. Automation | Are AI systems handling initial conversations? |
| 6. 24/7 Availability | Can people reach us after hours? |
| 7. Upsell & Bundle | Are we maximizing value per visit? |

**Your bottleneck is wherever people are falling out of your funnel.** Find it. Plug it. Then move to the next one.

Don't try to fix everything at once. Find the BIGGEST leak, plug it, then move downstream.

That's how you build a clinic that doesn't just survive — it scales.
