# Example Trainings

Two complete training presentations demonstrating WhisperCanvas output.

## Trainings

### 1. Plug Your Bottlenecks
**Topic:** Marketing funnel optimization for clinic owners
**Frames:** 9 (8 regular + 1 clinical sidebar)
**Style:** Glass Morphism (isometric 3D, frosted glass cards)

Teaches the concept of identifying and fixing bottlenecks in your patient acquisition funnel — from awareness to conversion to retention.

### 2. The Knee Is the Victim
**Topic:** Kinetic chain dysfunction and knee pain (biomechanics)
**Frames:** 8 (7 regular + 1 clinical sidebar)
**Style:** Glass Morphism with 3D anatomical models

Research-backed training on how hip weakness and ankle stiffness cause knee pain. Includes 12 cited research sources, assessment protocols, and treatment frameworks.

## Structure

Each training folder contains:
- `canvas.excalidraw` — Open in VS Code/Cursor with the Excalidraw extension
- `training.md` — Full script with research citations and action items
- `frames/` — Individual AI-generated images used in the canvas
