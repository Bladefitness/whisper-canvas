# The Clinic Owner's Growth System — Training Series

A 5-part training series designed to take clinic owners from "running ads and hoping" to having a systematic, full-funnel patient acquisition machine.

## Training Order

These trainings build on each other. Start with Training 1 and work through sequentially.

### Training 1: Plug Your Bottlenecks
**The foundation.** Understand the 7 stages of your patient acquisition system and identify where people are falling out of your funnel.

- [Training Script](01-plug-your-bottlenecks/training.md) (~15 min read / video)
- [Visual Canvas](01-plug-your-bottlenecks/canvas.excalidraw) (9 frames)

**Key concepts:** Virtuous cycle, reputation first, speed to lead, automation, 24/7 availability, upsell & bundle

---

### Training 2: The 3-5% Rule (TOFU / MOFU / BOFU)
**The insight that changes everything.** Only 3-5% of your market is ready to buy at any given time. Learn why full-funnel marketing beats "Book Now" ads.

- [Training Script](02-tofu-mofu-bofu/training.md) (~15 min read / video)
- [Visual Canvas](02-tofu-mofu-bofu/canvas.excalidraw) (7 frames)

**Key concepts:** Buyer's Pyramid, 95:5 Rule, Schwartz's 5 Levels of Awareness, content ratio, speed to lead multiplier

---

### Training 3: The Grand Slam Offer
**Build offers so good people feel stupid saying no.** The Value Equation, Value Stack, Godfather Offer, and risk reversal.

- [Training Script](03-grand-slam-offer/training.md) (~17 min read / video)
- [Visual Canvas](03-grand-slam-offer/canvas.excalidraw) (7 frames)

**Key concepts:** Value Equation (4 levers), Value Stack, Godfather → Grand Slam flow, guarantee types, naming formula, Starving Crowd

---

### Training 4: The Lead Machine
**The Core Four framework for getting patients.** Warm outreach, cold outreach, free content, and paid ads — and when to use each.

- [Training Script](04-the-lead-machine/training.md) (~17 min read / video)
- [Visual Canvas](04-the-lead-machine/canvas.excalidraw) (6 frames)

**Key concepts:** Core Four grid, 100 Outreach Method, Give/Give/Give/Ask, Content Machine, More/Better/New scaling, ad ROI math

---

### Training 5: The Ad Playbook
**How to run ads that actually make money.** Meta's Andromeda update, creative testing frameworks, campaign structure, and Google Ads.

- [Training Script](05-the-ad-playbook/training.md) (~20 min read / video)
- [Visual Canvas](05-the-ad-playbook/canvas.excalidraw) (7 frames)

**Key concepts:** Andromeda algorithm shift, Advantage+ structure, 6 Angles x 5 Hooks matrix, creative mix, TOFU/MOFU/BOFU ad funnel, weekly testing cycle

---

## Clinical Sidebars

Each training includes **clinical medicine analogies** that connect marketing concepts to clinical thinking:

| Training | Clinical Concept | Marketing Application |
|----------|-----------------|----------------------|
| 1 | Triage (Red/Yellow/Green) | Lead prioritization by urgency |
| 1 | Vital Signs | Business KPIs (CPL, CTR, conversion rate) |
| 2 | Sensitivity vs. Specificity | TOFU (wide net) vs. BOFU (precise targeting) |
| 2 | Pre-test Probability & PPV | Why nurtured leads convert better |
| 3 | NNT (Number Needed to Treat) | NNR (Number Needed to Reach) |
| 3 | Dose-Response Curve | Ad spend therapeutic window |
| 4 | Differential Diagnosis | Revenue problem diagnosis |
| 4 | Positive Predictive Value | Warm vs. cold lead conversion |
| 5 | Evidence-Based Medicine | Evidence-based marketing (levels of evidence) |
| 5 | Relative Risk | Full-funnel vs. BOFU-only ROI comparison |

These sidebars are visually distinct in the Excalidraw canvases — dashed red borders with light red backgrounds.
